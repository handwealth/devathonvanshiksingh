Frequently Asked Questions

Who can contribute?

Everyone with a passion for technology and innovation is welcome to contribute! Whether you're a student, a professional, or a hobbyist, we encourage you to participate.

Where can we reach out in case of queries?

For any queries, please reach out to us at support@devcation.com or join our community forum at forum.devcation.com.

Do I need to have coding experience to participate?

While some coding experience can be helpful, it's not mandatory. We have tracks and challenges for all skill levels, and mentorship will be provided.

What happens after the hackathon ends?

After the hackathon ends, the submitted projects will be reviewed by our judges. Finalists will be selected to pitch their ideas, and winners will be announced at the closing ceremony.

How can I stay updated on hackathon announcements and events?

Stay updated by following our social media channels and subscribing to our newsletter. All important announcements and event details will be shared there.
